About Us
TLC: The Learning Court is located in the Municipality of Consolacion, Cebu. Providing outstanding education to children in their formative years. TLC provides a unique high quality early learning experience for students ages 2-6 years old. The philosophy behind the curriculum is that children should be involved actively in their own learning through play. They “learn by doing”, often working with hands-on, open ended materials and carrying out projects of their own choosing, supported by highly qualified teachers with the above standard ratio of supervision. TLC approach encompasses all aspects of the child development and places focus on learning early skills, language, listening and literacy taught throughout the whole curriculum. TLC also believes that childhood is the most significant of a persons well being and of their becoming in the future. Thus, the education of our students are not confined to the four walls of the classroom. TLC enhance the curriculum with field trips (dentist clinic, library/museum, zoo, grocery, etc.), making students more involved in the community they are part of. At The Learning Court we work together with parents to support our children through their early years by developing their multiple intelligence; extending their emotional, social, academic, physical skills and abilities. We also introduce basics financial management skills.
